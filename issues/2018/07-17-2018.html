Hello Readers,


Chain React 2018 is in the books! (And soon, up on YouTube ). If you missed it, there's a few posts about it in this newsletter and the video will be available in a few weeks, so hopefully by Issue #65. 🤞

This issue has many posts and highlights as well as a new React Native version! 
One of the modules has been around awhile, react-native-video, but it's included again because I had the opportunity to talk with its new maintainer, Hampton Maxwell, who's done some major upgrades since taking it over. If you need to add video to your app or want to play with a cool module give it a try or, better yet, become a contributor to the library - PRs are very welcome!

P.S. I was glad to meet so many fans of the newsletter and React Native and to hear how much they enjoy it, especially one of our speakers, Ori Harel from Capriza, who told me his team stops and reads the newsletter as soon as it comes out So enjoy this one Ori and all our subscribers!
Executive Editor,
Frank von Hoven at Infinite Red
