We have an exciting new announcement!

The popular React Native Radio podcast is now a part of Infinite Red, the company behind this newsletter. This weekly podcast focuses on everything React Native, including deeper technical subjects as well as high-level longer term topics.

We have an all-new hosting panel:
  • Jamon Holmgren (CTO of Infinite Red)
  • Robin Heinze (senior software engineer at Infinite Red)
  • Adhithi Ravichandran (Pluralsight Author and React Native developer)
  • Harris Robin Kalash (React Native developer and startup founder)
We plan to have a variety of guests, including many who are maybe not as well-known in the community but who are doing very interesting things.

To listen to the first two episodes, go to ReactNativeRadio.com.
